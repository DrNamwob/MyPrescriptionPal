Frequently Asked Questions

Does MyPrescriptionPal collect my data?

No. The app runs entirely on your device. Your photos and prescription information are never sent to any server, stored externally, or shared with anyone — including us.

Why can't the app read my prescription label?

For best results, hold your phone steady in good lighting and make sure the full label is visible. Try taking the photo from a few different angles. The app works best with clearly printed text on flat surfaces.

What devices are supported?

MyPrescriptionPal requires an iPhone or iPad running iOS 16 or later.

Is the app free?

Yes, MyPrescriptionPal is completely free to use with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Still need help?

If you have a question, found a bug, or want to request a feature, reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
